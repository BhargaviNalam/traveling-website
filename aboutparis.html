About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, and culture. Famous for its café culture, landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, and world-class museums, Paris is a city that captivates millions of visitors every year.

Top Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The iconic symbol of Paris, the Eiffel Tower offers stunning views of the city from its observation decks.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris, housing thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture, Notre-Dame Cathedral is known for its intricate sculptures and stunning stained glass windows.

Champs-Élysées

One of the most famous avenues in the world, the Champs-Élysées is lined with shops, theaters, and cafés, stretching from the Arc de Triomphe to the Place de la Concorde.
